Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are there any lateness allowance?

A: Yes, you're allowed to turn assigments late. The maximum allowed lateness is 200 hours per semester. Be careful! It will affect your grade

Q: What will happen if students cheat?

A: The punishments will depend on the severity of the case, however all cheating students will have to attend session with the principal

Q: Can student turn assignments in using other ways (not using the LMS)?

A: Generally no, unless students have difficulties or technical problems and need to talk to the teacher first.
